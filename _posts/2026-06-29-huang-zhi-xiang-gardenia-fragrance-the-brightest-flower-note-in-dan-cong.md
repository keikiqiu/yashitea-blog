Huang Zhi Xiang (Gardenia Fragrance): The Brightest Flower Note in Dan Cong is one of those subjects where the more you dig, the more you find.

One thing that caught my attention is it is one of the “top ten” Dan Cong aromas, but it tastes nothing like the heavy, perfumed florals you might expect..

If you need background on tea ceremony, the [Wikipedia article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_tea_ceremony) is a good starting point.

Another angle worth considering: the gardenia note is there, but it is backed by a sweetness like fresh sugarcane and a finish so crisp it feels like biting into a green apple..

I hope sharing these notes on huang zhi xiang (gardenia fragrance): the brightest flower note in dan cong gives you a useful starting point.
